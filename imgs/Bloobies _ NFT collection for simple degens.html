What the fuck are bloobies?

Bloobies are 2000 unique NFTs living on the ethereum blockchain. It is a PFP ready, art-focused collectible project for simple degens. Bloobies is a love letter to the NFT community. The fact that a diverse set of people, from around the globe, came together to build the future inspired us to launch our own collection.

Can you talk about the art?

The art for this project is 3D and generative. If you know @tippydreamer, then you know it was going to be something like this. All the body shapes, facial expressions, mouth elements and backgrounds have been made individually. The backgrounds, on the other hand, are 2D and were hand-drawn by the talented @nabiharauf.

How do I mint a bloobies NFT?

You can go to the mint page: mint.bloobies.art to get your bloobies. It is completely free to mint a bloobie. You just need some ETH in your wallet to pay the gas. We're supporting three types of wallets on our site: MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, and Wallet Connect. A single wallet can only mint 5 NFTs per transaction.

Why don't you have a roadmap?

We don't want to over-hype and then under-deliver. Our strategy is the opposite: launch first, and then hype later. This project is for simple degens only. If you like what you see, then press that mint button ⎯ or don't. We can't tell you what to do. Also did we mention that it's completely free?
